Everyone

Love awakens

This is the first short movie by the italian director Tommi Boom. Produced along various months of hard work, with nearly zero budget or experience, the film tells the story of a young boy with a special addiction: sleep. Full of metaphorical messages, the plot aims to move the viewer and invite him to act.
